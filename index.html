Projects

Elena Zaharova’s Portfolio & Blog

elenazaharova.com

Elena decorates shop windows and spaces: her goal is for the shops to welcome people in, place each handbag on its very spot and make sure visitors have a great time. We’ve made a site-portfolio and a blog where Elena writes about her travels, book recommendations, shoe reviews and work process.

Design and development: Artur Paikin; Art-director: Elena Zaharova; Logo: Eugene Volkov

Local Food

locallocal.ru

Local Food organizes food markets, makes a food box from products supplied by the local farmers and offers a coworking space for the aspiring food entrepreneurs to rent. On the website you can learn more about each of their projects, submit an application or order a food box.

Development: Artur Paikin; Design: Maria Milavina

Sergey Korol’s Blog

sergeykorol.ru/blog

Sergey Korol blogs about books, traveling and unusual things. At Baguette we’ve redesigned the blog templates: the font and line height has been increased, text size adjusts to the screen, so now readers are able to enjoy new posts on any device, wherever they are. Graphics is adapted for Retina-displays. The layout features a side column and occasional excerpts from the content.

Design and development: Artur Paikin

Arsenicum — Dmitry Loginov’s design atelier & studio

arsenicum.co.uk

Dmitry Loginov is a Russian fashion designer, the author of the Arsenicum brand, known for its vivid images and extraordinary show-displays of new collections. We have developed a website that unites all Arsenicum’s collections since 2005. Each collection features a soundtrack that you can listen to while browsing.

An Interview with Dmitry Loginov at The Village newspaper (in Russian).

Design and development: Artur Paikin; Art-director: Dmitry Loginov; Manager: Anna Kudashkina

Tipi Coffee

coffee.the-tipi.com

Tipi Coffee is a pop-up coffee shop. It appears on local food markets and events to bring the best fresh coffee to customers. We have designed and implemented a style and identity for the coffee shop: from the logo and price tag to mugs and pies. We update the Facebook page, brew slow-coffee, bake muffins and cakes.

Idea and logo: Arutr Paikin; Decoration: Elena Zaharova; Coffee roasting: Double B
