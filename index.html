Baguette

Project bureau by Artur Paikin

We make websites and applications. While focusing on frontend development, we also do design, backend and bake muffins. Email us, follow our work and check out selected projects:

  • Lettera

    lettera.co

    Lettera tells you about one book worth your attention every week, and then let’s you purchase it right there in two clicks. For Kindle, iOS, Android or microwaves, DRM free and all. We’ve build all the templates and styles for Rails app that powers Lettera to make it look and work great.

    • Frontend development: Artur Paikin
    • Project lead: Egor Eremeev
    • Backend & Design: Lettera team

  • Koti Home Automation System

    github.com/arturi/kotihome

    Home automation system based on Arduino with sensors, Raspberri Pi, Node.js, Express, MQTT and Socket.io. Features an extendable React control panel and a friendly robot that you can talk to over microphone or chat via Telegram Bot API.

    • Design & Development: Artur Paikin

  • Bike to Work

    bike2work.ru

    Website for Bike to Work days in Russia. Started in Moscow, now spread throughout the country, with roughly over 10,000 participants. The admin interface allows to add and customize page blocks like Instagram feeds or columns and buttons, and even add cities with their own subdomain. It is also the first time something we’ve worked on has been featured on billboards in subway and throughout on the city, feeling good.

    • Design & Development: Artur Paikin
    • Art director: Vladimir Kumov

  • Serebro Nabora

    serebronabora.com

    Annual international type conference held in Moscow. Responsive website with support for two languages and a flexible admin interface that helps easily alter conference programm, rearrange content blocks and update information.

    • Development: Artur Paikin
    • Design: Vyacheslav Kyrylenko
    • Art director: Gayaneh Bagdasaryan

  • Elena Zaharova

    elenazaharova.com

    Elena decorates shop windows and spaces: her goal is for the shops to welcome people in, place each handbag on its very spot and make sure visitors have a great time. We’ve made a site-portfolio and a blog where Elena writes about her travels, book recommendations, shoe reviews and work process.

    • Design & Development: Artur Paikin
    • Art director: Elena Zaharova
    • Lettering: Eugene Volkov
    • Photographer: Alexander Utkin

  • Label Maker

    arturi.github.io/labelmaker

    A plugin that lets you put markers on pictures that expand into comment bubbles when clicked or hovered. Responsive. Stores data as an HTML list (ul or ol) to make it accessible and indexable.

    • Design & Development: Artur Paikin

  • Made by Sloths

    An online store that sells stickers and illustrations by designers from all over the world. Powered by Shopify.

    • Development: Artur Paikin
    • Design: Maria Milavina

  • Sergey Korol

    sergeykorol.ru/blog

    Sergey Korol blogs about books, traveling, vintage cameras and unusual things. At Baguette we’ve redesigned blog templates for the best readability on any screen. Features: full screen images on click, image sliders and embeds, side notes and standouts from the main text.

    • Design & Development: Artur Paikin

  • Local Food

    locallocal.ru

    Local Food organizes food markets, sells a food box full of products supplied by the local farmers and offers a coworking space for the aspiring food entrepreneurs. On the website you can learn more about each of their projects, submit an application or order a food box.

    • Development: Artur Paikin
    • Design: Maria Milavina
